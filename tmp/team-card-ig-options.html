Team Card — IG Section Options

Kelly has two Instagram handles; the other artists have one. Three ways to fix the height mismatch.

Option A

Side-by-side handles (smaller text on Kelly)

Both handles share one row. Kelly's row uses slightly smaller text (10.5px vs 12px) and a vertical divider. All four cards stay the same height. Tradeoff: Kelly's handles read a touch smaller than the others.
