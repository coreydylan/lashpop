**Scope:** Explains "(Desktop) refresh glitches and kicks you down to gallery section" + "(Mobile) refresh glitches" + frosted-header flicker.

1. Ship the cheap wins first (in §1 just-do-it): drop `scroll-behavior:smooth`, hash-strip + referrer gate in SectionHashDeepLink
2. Get Corey's call on Q12 (SSR re-enable vs min-h placeholders vs defer) and Q17 (mobile scroll restore yes/no)
3. Apply mobile scroll-position persistence to `.mobile-scroll-container` if Q17=yes
4. Verify MobileHeader frosted-glass flicker resolves automatically

**Risk:** Medium overall (low for cheap wins, medium for mobile scroll restore which is new behavior)

**Files in scope:** `src/app/LandingPageV2Client.tsx`, `src/app/globals.css`, `src/lib/smoothScroll.ts`, `src/components/landing-v2/MobileHeader.tsx`

**Primary files:** `src/app/LandingPageV2Client.tsx`, `src/lib/smoothScroll.ts`, `src/app/globals.css`, `src/components/landing-v2/MobileHeader.tsx`, `src/components/sections/Navigation.tsx`

**Primary files:** `src/components/landing-v2/sections/FounderLetterSection.tsx`, `src/types/founder-letter.ts`, `src/components/landing-v2/MobileHeroBackground.tsx`, `src/app/LandingPageV2Client.tsx`, `src/app/admin/content/founder-letter/FounderLetterEditor.tsx`

### Booking process
Single funnel: homepage step 2 cards → ServiceBrowserModal (BrowseView) → DetailView (the price/time/description middle step Corey wants gone) → BookingView (Vagaro iframe) → BookingConfirmation. ### Vagaro sync architecture
Vagaro sync runs as a Cloudflare Worker on cron 3x/day (`workers/vagaro-sync`) — with an unscheduled Next.js fallback at `/api/cron/sync-vagaro-services` and a webhook at `/api/webhooks/vagaro`. The real cross-cutting problem: several pieces of the site depend on local enrichment columns (categoryId, subcategoryId) the sync never sets, and on photos/descriptions the sync clobbers or omits. Quiz result-screen photos don't sync from Vagaro by design. Two near-identical sync implementations that will drift.
