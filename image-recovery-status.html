LashPop — Photo Library Recovery Status

After our image host (Cloudflare R2) was taken offline, the entire LashPop photo library went down. We rebuilt it from backups and the old website. Over 130 photos were recovered and are live again — including the full portfolios for Evie Ells and Emily Rogers, and one of Rachel Edwards' shots pulled back from the old website.

The photos below are the ones still missing. Almost all of them only ever lived on an artist's phone (they were uploaded straight from each artist's camera roll), so we need them re-sent. For each one we've listed the file name, what it is, and our best read on where the original is.

Totals: 32 photos to re-send  ·  2 that can't be recovered (need a new photo). On the live site, every missing slot now shows a tasteful "Photo coming soon" placeholder so nothing looks broken.
